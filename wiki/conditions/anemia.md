# Anemia

**First Noted:** 2026-06-06 via [[2026-06-06_CBC]]

## Description
Microcytic hypochromic anemia diagnosed based on CBC results (Hb: 9.4 g/dl, low PCV, RBC, MCH, MCHC, and high RDW). It is likely secondary to blood loss from PV bleeding associated with [[Uterine Fibroids]].

## Key Lab Values
- **2026-06-06:** Haemoglobin 9.4 g/dl

## Status & Management
- **Active.** Aggressive iron supplementation (potentially IV iron if oral is not tolerated) is strongly recommended to combat the significant blood loss (Hb 9.4 g/dl), alongside treating the root cause of the bleeding.
