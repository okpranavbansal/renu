# Disorders of Thyroid Gland

**First Noted in Wiki:** 2026-06-07 via [[insurance_details.md]]

## Description
A declared pre-existing condition (PED) noted in the Niva Bupa Health Recharge insurance policy. Specific details of the thyroid disorder (e.g., Hypothyroidism vs. Hyperthyroidism) are not documented in the current medical records.

## Insurance Implication
Subject to a 36-month waiting period under the Niva Bupa Health Recharge policy. Full coverage for this condition starts **July 31, 2028**.

## Clinical Implications & Management
- **The "Root Cause" Link:** Thyroid dysfunction (specifically Hypothyroidism) is a well-documented driver of heavy menstrual bleeding and can exacerbate bleeding from [[uterine_fibroids.md]]. It is also linked to secondary [[hypertension.md]].
- **Diagnostics in Progress:** Dr. Mittal ordered a **S.TSH** (Serum TSH) test on 2026-06-06 to investigate this exact link and determine if managing the thyroid might resolve the multisystem symptoms (see [[2026-06-06_Mittals_Clinic_Visit]]).
